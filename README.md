# Super Message Sanctuary

Welcome to your new messaging app. This app is designed to be simple, fast, and secure.

## How to Install the App (Android)

To get this app on your phone as a real app (not just a preview), follow these steps:

1. **Build the APK**: 
   - Open your terminal and run: `npm run build:android`.
   - This will start a process using Expo Application Services (EAS).
   - You may need to log in to an Expo account.

2. **Download the File**:
   - Once the build is finished, you will receive a download link in your terminal.
   - Open that link on your Android phone.
   - Download the `.apk` file.

3. **Install on Phone**:
   - Open the downloaded file.
   - Your phone might say "Install from unknown sources" - click **Allow** or **Settings** to enable this for your browser.
   - Click **Install**.

4. **Open and Use**:
   - You will now find "Super Message" in your app list.
   - Open it, enter your phone number, and start messaging!

## Features
- **Simple Login**: Just your phone number and an optional PIN.
- **SMS Backup**: Automatically saves your text messages.
- **Real-time Chat**: Talk to other app users instantly.
- **Clean Design**: A beautiful, white interface that is easy on the eyes.
